About Proxy Shift

We help companies get their shift together

We have 8 years of experience in retail, in-patient and out-patient pharmacies. Whether it's a last minute callout or planned time off we know firsthand how unnecessarily time consuming and labor intensive it is to fill an empty shift.

  • Last minute callouts are unavoidable - Taking time out of an already busy day to reach someone to cover an empty shift is both time and resource limited. Proxy Shift eliminates the hassle of trying to manually reach someone by notifying every qualified employee instantly. This allows employees, managers, and schedulers to focus on the task at hand and not scramble around looking for contact information.
  • Finding coverage for planned openings (e.g. vacations and maternity leave) are even more time consuming - Sending out shift availability lists through email or fax results in duplicate requests for the same opening, poor response rates, and is overall a tedious time consuming task. Our Open Shift page shows - in real time- only shifts that are available. If someone covers a shift it is automatically removed from the list - no more duplicates or back and forth between employees.
